# Lead Acquisition Strategy: 500+ High-Intent Leads

## PRIMARY LEAD SOURCES

### 1. Database Procurement (FASTEST PATH)

Purchase verified contact lists from reputable providers:

**Dental Clinics:**

- **SphereScout.io**: 242,000 dentists, 47K emails, 151K phones (decision-makers focused)
- **Core List USA**: 63,000 dental clinic contacts with emails/phones
- **Dental Lists**: 170K active dentists, 110K verified emails

**Law Firms:**

- **Justia Directory**: Free scraping (practice area + location filters)
- **Martindale-Hubbell**: Premium directory, scrapable
- **State Bar Associations**: Free, official lists by state
- **Cost**: $300-$600 for 5,000-10,000 attorney contacts

**ACTION:** Purchase 200 dental + 200 law firm contacts immediately to begin outreach.

### 2. Google My Business Scraping

Extract business data from Google Maps/GMB listings.

**Tools:**

- Outscraper.com ($49/mo for 5,000 credits)
- PhantomBuster ($69/mo)
- Apify.com ($49/mo)

**Data Points:**

- Business name
- Phone number
- Website
- Address
- Reviews (identify pain points)
- Business hours

**Search Queries:**

- "dental clinic near [city]"
- "family dentist [state]"
- "personal injury lawyer [city]"
- "estate planning attorney [city]"

**Tier 1 Qualification:**

- 10+ reviews mentioning "hard to reach" or "long wait times"
- Phone number prominently displayed on GMB
- Business hours listed (gaps = missed calls)

**ACTION:** Scrape 300 dental clinics and 200 law firms in top 20 metro areas.

---

### 3. Website Contact Form Mining

Visit practice websites and extract contact info from "Contact Us" pages.

**Indicators of High Intent:**

- "Call us 24/7" messaging (but only lists business hours)
- "We answer every call" claims (shows awareness of problem)
- Multiple phone numbers (busy practice)
- "Emergency appointments available"

**Tools:**

- Hunter.io (email finder)
- Clearbit (firmographic data)
- Manual extraction for quality

**ACTION:** Cross-reference GMB leads with websites. Enrich top 100 leads.

---

### 4. LinkedIn Prospecting

**Titles to Target:**

- Dental: Practice Owner, Office Manager, Practice Administrator, DDS

**LinkedIn Sales Navigator Filters:**

- Industry: Dental Practices, Law Practice

**ACTION:** Connect with 100 dental and 100 law decision-makers.

## LEAD TIER CLASSIFICATION

### Tier 1: Perfect Fit (Immediate Outreach)

- 5+ employees
- Prominent phone number on website/GMB
- Reviews mentioning missed calls, long waits, or poor communication
- Active on Google Ads or Facebook Ads
- Located in metro area (high call volume)
- Specialty practices (cosmetic dental, PI law = high ticket)

**Expected Close Rate:** 5-10%

---

### Tier 2: Good Fit (Nurture Campaign)

- 2-5 employees
- Phone-based business (visible on website)
- Some online reviews
- Not currently advertising aggressively
- Suburban/secondary markets

**Expected Close Rate:** 2-5%

---

### Tier 3: Long-Term (Drip Campaign)

- Solo practitioners
- Minimal online presence
- Low review count
- Rural markets
- May not have budget yet (but will grow)

**Expected Close Rate:** 1-2%

## LEAD ORGANIZATION

### CRM Setup

Use **HubSpot Free** or **Airtable** to track leads.

**Fields:**

- Lead name
- Business name
- Contact info (phone, email)
- Industry (dental, law, HVAC, etc.)
- Tier (1, 2, 3)
- Source (GMB, database, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Outreach status (not contacted, email sent, call made, meeting booked)
- Notes (pain points mentioned, objections, etc.)

### Segmentation for Outreach

- **Dental Tier 1**: Cold call first (dental owners prefer phone)
- **Law Tier 1**: Email first (attorneys prefer written communication)
- **All Tier 2**: Email sequence + LinkedIn message
- **All Tier 3**: Drip campaign (1 email/month)
